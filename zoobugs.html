Zoobugs

Touch-based adaptive kiosk games to support learning and SDK for developing interactive multimedia experience on kiosks.

In collaboration with the Lincoln Children's Zoo and the Department of Computer Science, University of Nebraska-Lincoln, I designed and programmed a library of reusable interface components by hand in Runtime Revolution. This was my first time using Runtime Revolution, implementing UI components from scratch, and working with SQLite.

I was drawn to this project because it offered potential to embed artificial intelligence in the design of interactive components on touchscreens to support learning. I saw it as a chance to further develop my skills developing for touchscreens that I started building in my previous work.
