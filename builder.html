Builder

High-quality DIY projects with great assembly instructions.

GitHub, Gitter

Builder is a tool that helps creators organize do-it-yourself electronics, software, and product design projects into complete presentaions and kits. At the heart of Builder is a mechanism to generate personalized step-by-step assembly instructions. For electronics, this eliminates the need to reference documentation for hardware, choose pins to use, and figure out how to connect components to a controller chip (e.g., Builder boards, Raspberry Pi, Arduino). I decided to do this to take a small step toward making it possible for more individuals and communities to participate directly in technology innovation. This is also a problem I encounter every time I work on electronics projects.

Example Usage

To generate instructions to connect an IR rangefinder and servo to the Raspberry Pi 3, users would execute the following command:

builder connect --model raspberry-pi-3 --model machineee/generic-servo --model ir-rangefinder --model ir-rangefinder
