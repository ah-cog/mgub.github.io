Connected Expression is a workshop focused on creatively combining computation and materials to connect elements of the physical and digital worlds. The workshop will involve exploratory design and rapid prototyping with computer-based tools, including graphical and textual programming languages for generating sound and image with electronic sensors.

The workshop will include presentations about designing computational media for playful learning and discussions about using these tools and concepts with a wide range of learners in creative learning environments. The workshop will culminate in an informal exhibit of installations or performances open to the public.
