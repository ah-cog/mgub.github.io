My work explores the intersection of creative thought, physical form, and computation. I try to understand how to design tools that enable people to leverage the intuitive knowledge they gain from their embodied experiences of the physical world for making interactive systems and defining their behavior, rather than technical languages and conventional knowledge as in traditional circuitry and programming.
