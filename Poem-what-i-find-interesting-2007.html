my math professor notices which numbers people choose.
he asked for numbers to use in his example,
and was seemingly intrigued when he noticed
that no negatives or zero were chosen.
he speaks of math as a language and a tool to represent thought.
he probably finds the real world quite scary.
i wonder if he finds comfort in numbers.
an acquaintance of mine seems to notice things about words.
she appreciates the shape of them,
and how they appear or function with one another,
and their interesting or artistic use.
she speaks of words and their meaning as different things.
she must have thought about words a lot.
people find things interesting.
