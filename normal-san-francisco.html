Normal San Francisco

This video was an accident. I didn't intend to make it. Making it bordered on remembering something from a few hours ago.

The scene sequencing, selection, editing, and sound were done automatically without my knowledge by algorithms started by Boomarang and Google Photos. To make this film, I walked around to occasionally stand in one place to frame the shot. I stood for about 5 seconds each time before moving on.
